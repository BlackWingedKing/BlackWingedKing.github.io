## About the Book

This is a very old book (1968) by [Alexander R Luria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_Luria) - neuropsychologist, often credited as the father of modern [neuropsychological assessment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neuropsychological_assessment).

This book is more of a psychologist's record of a patient. A person who has infinite or immeasurable memory, who he describes as Mr. S. Mainly answers the question how his emotional life was?
