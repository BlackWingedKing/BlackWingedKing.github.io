## The COVID-19 Piano Journey

So, during this COVID-19 break, I joined a crash course at my hometown (I was only expecting it to last for 10 days. How short sighted was I?). Anyway my lessons got started and Our PM Modi announces junta curfew. The next day I went out with my dad and bought this [Casio keyboard](https://www.amazon.in/Casio-CTX700-Sensitive-Portable-Keyboard/dp/B0794RNK5V). The day after that complete lockdown was announced. I thought to myself, "How lucky am I?". But the main question that remains at this point is how am I gonna learn keyboard without an Instructor. So, I'm documenting here my efforts to learn piano under the supervision of internet sensei.

I found out [this](https://courses.pianovideolessons.com/) website has some real good piano lessons. So, I'm following this one.
